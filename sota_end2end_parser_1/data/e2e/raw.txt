Li Huiling once left Hong Kong to go to Canada, served as a reporter in the Ming Pao in Canada. After returning to Hong Kong in 1990, she continued to work for the Ming Pao. Before the Chief Executive of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Election four years later, she had been promoted from reporter, political editor and news Officer to News Director. When she interviewed Rhodeson exclusively, she disclosed the news that he wanted to participate in the inaugural  chief executive election.
